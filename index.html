Shanghai - China's Biggest City

Apart from being the biggest city in China, Shanghai is also known to be China's "most populous city". It is China's fastest growing city that also has a lot of influence from the Western cultures.

Shanghai has an amazing city life, more especially the night life. There is an amazing contrast between ancient Chinese architecture and modern architecture that makes this a city worth visiting.

The food in Shanghai is most definitely also a must-try! Soup dumplings as well as steamed crab are just some of the delicious Chinese foods that are worth trying during a visit to the city of Shanghai.
